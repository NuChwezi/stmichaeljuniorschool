Welcome to St. Michael Junior School Nyange, a mixed day and boarding primary school located in Mateete Parish, Sembabule District, along the Masaka-Mbarara Road, 12 km from Mbirizi Town. Our school is committed to providing high quality education at an affordable fee.

Our Mission

To provide pupils with the educational tools, spiritual and moral values that they need to become responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To offer affordable quality education.

Our Values

  • Quality Education: We are dedicated to providing high-quality education that meets the needs of our students.
  • Affordability: We believe that quality education should be accessible to all, which is why we offer affordable fees.
  • Spiritual Growth: We nurture the spiritual growth of our students, helping them develop a strong sense of purpose and values.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community and belonging among our students, parents, and staff.

Why Choose Us

  • Experienced Teachers: Our teachers are experienced and dedicated to providing quality education.
  • Conducive Learning Environment: Our school provides a conducive learning environment that promotes academic excellence.
  • Affordable Fees: We offer affordable fees, making quality education accessible to all.
